Top 5 Best AI Influencer Creator Apps in 2025

By Infu AI Team · February 2025 · 8 min read

The AI influencer industry is booming. Virtual influencers like Lil Miquela and Aitana Lopez have proven that AI-generated characters can build massive followings and generate real revenue. But you no longer need a team of 3D artists and developers to create one.

In 2025, several apps make it possible to create your own AI influencer directly from your phone. We've tested and compared the top options to help you choose the best tool for your needs.

What to Look for in an AI Influencer Creator App

Before diving into our picks, here are the key features that matter most:

  • Character Quality: How realistic and unique are the generated characters?
  • Consistency: Can you use the same character across multiple images and scenes?
  • Customization: Camera angles, facial expressions, scenes, and style options
  • Ease of Use: How quickly can you create content?
  • Pricing: Value for money compared to features offered

Top 5 AI Influencer Creator Apps

1. Infu AI — Best Overall AI Influencer Creator

⭐ 4.8/5 — Editor's Choice

Infu AI stands out as the most comprehensive AI influencer creation tool available on iOS. What makes it unique is the level of control it gives you over your virtual characters.

Key Features:

  • Infinite scene generation (café, street, beach, nightlife, business)

Why We Love It: Infu AI is the only app that combines unlimited character creation, multiple camera angles, facial expression control, and scene variety in a single mobile app. The character consistency across different scenes is impressive — your AI influencer looks like the same person whether they're at a café or on the beach.

2. Glambase

⭐ 4.2/5

Glambase is a web-based platform focused on creating AI influencers for social media monetization. It offers character creation and some social media management features.

Key Features:

  • Web-based character creation
  • Social media integration
  • Monetization focused

Pricing: Varies by plan

Limitations: Web-only (no mobile app), less control over camera angles and expressions compared to Infu AI, fewer customization options for character poses.

3. Fanvue AI

⭐ 4.0/5

Fanvue offers AI character creation tools primarily focused on the creator economy and content monetization platforms.

Key Features:

  • AI character generation
  • Content monetization integration
  • Creator-focused tools

Pricing: Varies by plan

Limitations: More focused on a specific niche, fewer scene and angle options, limited character customization compared to dedicated creator apps.

4. Midjourney + Manual Workflow

⭐ 3.8/5

Some creators use general AI image generators like Midjourney to create influencer content. While the image quality is excellent, maintaining character consistency requires significant skill and effort.

Key Features:

  • Extremely high image quality
  • Flexible creative control via prompts

Pricing: Starting at $10/month

Limitations: No built-in character consistency. Requires extensive prompt engineering knowledge. Not designed specifically for influencer content. No mobile app — Discord-based workflow. Very steep learning curve.

5. Ready Player Me + AI Tools

⭐ 3.5/5

Ready Player Me creates 3D avatars that can be used across platforms. Combined with AI rendering tools, it can produce influencer-style content, but it requires a more technical setup.

Key Features:

  • 3D avatar creation
  • Cross-platform compatibility
  • Developer-friendly API

Pricing: Free tier available, paid plans for commercial use

Limitations: More suited for gaming/metaverse than social media influencer content. Requires technical knowledge. Output looks more like 3D animation than realistic photography.
