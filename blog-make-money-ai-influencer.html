Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not being transparent: Always disclose your influencer is AI-generated. Audiences respect honesty, and it can actually be a unique selling point.
  • Inconsistent posting: The algorithm rewards consistency. Post at least 5 times per week.
  • Ignoring engagement: Reply to every comment, especially in the early days. Build a community, not just a follower count.
  • Too many niches: Pick one niche and own it. A fashion AI influencer and a tech AI influencer are two different brands.
  • Giving up too early: Most AI influencer accounts take 3-6 months to gain traction. Be patient and consistent.
